Tacos baby!

Description

Tacos have got to be hands down one of the best foods out there. They can be made many different ways with many different ingredients, but the tacos we will be making today are grilled chicken tacos with slaw and pico de gayo.

If you are not a fan of chicken tacos, thats okay! This recipe works perfectly with grilled or fried fish as well pulled pork. Feel free to swap out ingredients accordingly.

Ingredients

  • Flour tortillas
  • 500g chicken thigh
  • 1 white onion
  • 1 tomato
  • 1 bunch of coriander
  • Lemon
  • Hot sauce

Steps

  1. Dice chicken into 2cm cubes
  2. Saute chicken in a hot fry pan
  3. Dice onion, tomato and coriander and place in a bowl. Add salt and the juice of half a lemon
  4. Heat tortilla for 20 seconds a side in a fry pan
  5. Place tortilla on plate and add chicken and pico de gayo
  6. Finish with hot sauce and optionally garnish with a few leaves of coriander
